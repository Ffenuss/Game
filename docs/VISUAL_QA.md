# Visual QA

## Android screenshot checklist

1. HUD does not cover the player.
2. HUD does not cover enemies.
3. Joystick does not dominate the screen.
4. Attack, dodge, interact, and heal buttons are reachable by thumbs.
5. Health and stamina remain readable.
6. The level fills the screen naturally.
7. No large gray debug-looking empty area is visible.
8. Bridge, path, and collision boundaries remain readable.
9. Checkpoint is visible.
10. Interact prompt is readable.
11. Dialogue panel is readable.
12. Pause, inventory, and journal are readable.
13. Pixel art is not blurry.
14. Performance remains acceptable.

## Known visual limitations

- Placeholder art is still intentionally simple and generated.
- Some world props are still temporary silhouettes.
- The current slice is visually improved, but not final-art complete.
